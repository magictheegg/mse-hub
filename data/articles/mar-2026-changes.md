### Ardleby Gatekeeper
<change-preview>

Gatekeeper presents a must-answer threat on turn 3 due to its ability to keep cloning itself to make an infinite stream of bodies. If it isn't removed at instant speed, it also generates immedate value, but because of how devastating it is to let ardleby live even a turn it forces the opponent to 2 for 1 themselves. If the opponent isn't able to immediately answer the gatekeeper, it creates a copy of itself and is further insulated from removal, requiring the opponent to spend multiple removal spells to stop the endless stream of bodies.

### Prophellos, Hungering Manipulator
<change-preview>
https://github.com/Voyager-MTG/Voyager-MTG.github.io/blob/007ff1efc5f9ee23b78d312f851d137aa04c2eb7/sets/FOE-files/img/15_Prophellos,%20Hungering%20Manipulator.jpg?raw=true
https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1337208262965203024/1469401079379660973/image.png?ex=69a1e3e5&is=69a09265&hm=3c1c7d4bdad0f099de57cd272b98c0fb31933110afb0a163b46bcb651eae5aa2&=&format=webp&quality=lossless&width=469&height=654
</change-preview>

Prophellos was simply one of the cheapest costed and easiest to break delve cards in both canon and custom. It could come down on 0 mana after an [[Algaja, Lord of Disregard]] and double your board presence, while also being able to sometimes come out of the graveyard. This change has been made to bring it more in line with the rest of the format (and canon) and prevent it from being as much of a timebomb.
